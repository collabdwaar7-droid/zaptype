## 📊 Why ZapType?

Most typing websites are outdated and limited.
ZapType combines **learning + testing + gaming + analytics** into one modern platform.

You can:

* Practice touch typing
* Test your speed
* Track your progress
* Improve accuracy
* Play typing games

All in one place.
